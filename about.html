About me

I'm a UI Designer currently based in Nürnberg, Germany.

Being creative is what fulfills me the most. I’m a very inquisitive person willing to always discover and learn new things, taking my inspiration from everywhere.

What I love most about Design is that it’s allowing me to be creative, at the same time motivating me to improve and learn new skills and techniques.

My design style tends to be minimalist. I strongly focus on an easy to understand and interact with interface, but I love to take care of details and I always like to add some creative elements that makes it stand out.

In my free time I love to spend it with family and friends, discover new places, read a good book or just enjoy a movie.

Overview

The apps attempt is to be a helpful tool in everyone’s kitchen. With that intention, I considered what could the needs of nowadays users, on these theme, be. Afterwards I conducted a user research to find out more about those needs and then based on the key findings I created the MVP objective. Only then I initiated the creative design process.

User Research

Starting from the hypothesis that people are trying to approach a different way of eating and are interested in a healthier diet, but don‘t have much time to spend in the kitchen room, and therefore are searching for fast and easy recipes, I conducted an user research to find out what are the most encountered problems and which solutions I have to provide.

Key Findings

  • Participants are not 100% satisfied with their way of eating and would like to eat healthier
  • Healthy but delicious recipes is what everyone is wishing for
  • Fast and easy to follow recipes are the winner
  • Photos/videos of the cooking process are also a big help when following a recipe

MVP and JTBD

Based on the findings from the user research I focused on the MVP objective and on the jobs to be done with their corresponding features. Focusing first on the user needs was a very important step in order to smoothly advance into the next steps of the project.

MVP Objective

The app will allow users to cook new dishes providing a wide variety of recipes and the necessary instructions.

Needed Features

  • Ingredient list
  • Preparation steps
  • Search tool
  • Filter tool
  • Organization system

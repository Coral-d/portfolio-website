Overview

Beauty Rescue is a native app that wants to be a helpful tool to health-conscious and wellness-oriented users. The app is for everyone who is interested in beauty products and self care, but wants to make thoughtful choices.

My Role

I was the UX and UI Designer of this project which included conducting a competitive analysis, user flow and testing, wireframing, prototyping and developing the finished interface design.

Purpose and Context

This was a Course Project focused on creating a native app for both iOS and Android based on their interface guidelines, respectively Human Interface Design for iOS and Material Design for Android.

My choice to create this particular beauty app was because I previously worked in the beauty industry and I know that many people are becoming more responsible and interested in knowing what are they really putting on their skin/hair and what are they paying for.

Objective

Since the users are becoming more conscious about the cosmetic products they are using, the major objective was to help them to easily understand what the ingredients of a product really mean and then organize those ingredients in a colorful system in order to create a more powerful visual effect.

Approach

After deciding what kind of app I want to create, I began to make some research about other similar apps on the market conducting a competitive analysis on them, identifying what design patterns I have to take into account and deciding what key features I want to introduce on my own app. At the same time I had to make sure that I design according the guidelines of both iOS and Android.
